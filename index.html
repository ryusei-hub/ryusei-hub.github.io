About me

Hi, my name is Ding-Gang Lin, but everyone calls me David! I recently graduated from the University of Manchester with a first class degree in BSc. Computer Science.

I was first exposed to the world of coding (starting with Python) during my time in secondary school. However, I believe that my passion for coding really started from when I decided to learn C# in the hopes of creating a turn-based RPG based on the popular video game franchise "Animal Crossing: New Horizons" in Unity.

After graduation, I now have experience in many different languages, such as Javascript, Haskell, C++, C, SQL and more. Of which, I am the most confident in Java and Python.

Aside from programming, I am also interested in learning languages. I taught myself Japanese and earned a qualification in the Japanese-Language Proficiency Test: N1 (the highest level achievable). Since I am Taiwanese and raised in the UK, this means that I can speak a total of three languages - English, Mandarin and Japanese.
